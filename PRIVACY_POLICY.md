# ROAM Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 18, 2026
**Contact:** privacy@roamapp.app

---

## Overview

ROAM — AI Travel Planner ("ROAM," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and what rights you have over your data. By using ROAM, you agree to the practices described here.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account information:** When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name.
- **Trip data:** Destination names, travel dates, budget range, travel preferences, and itineraries you generate.
- **Travel preferences:** Interests, travel style, vibes, and any other input you provide when planning a trip.
- **Journal entries:** Any diary entries or notes you write within the app.
- **Savings goals:** Destination wish lists and savings targets you set in the Dream tab.

### Information We Collect Automatically

- **Usage data:** Which features you use, how often you generate itineraries, and general interaction patterns within the app.
- **Device information:** Device type, operating system version, and app version. We do not collect your device's unique hardware identifier.
- **Crash and error reports:** Anonymized diagnostic information when the app encounters an error, used only to fix bugs.

### Location Information

- **Optional.** ROAM requests location access only when you use features that require it (such as I Am Here Now). We do not collect or store your precise location history. Location data is used in real time to generate relevant recommendations and is not retained on our servers after the request is fulfilled.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Generate personalized travel itineraries through CRAFT mode
- Deliver destination-specific data including weather, safety info, visa requirements, and emergency contacts
- Power your Trip Wrapped summaries and Journal
- Manage your subscription and process payments
- Send you trip reminders and anniversary notifications (only if you enable them)
- Improve the app based on anonymized usage patterns
- Respond to support requests

We do not use your data to train AI models without your explicit consent.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

ROAM uses the following third-party services to deliver its features. Each service operates under its own privacy policy.

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Supabase** | Database and authentication | Account info, trip data, chat messages |
| **Anthropic Claude** | AI itinerary generation (via server-side proxy) | Trip preferences, destination, budget (no PII) |
| **Perplexity** | Live AI-powered destination search | Destination name, query text |

| **OpenWeather** | Weather forecasts for destinations | Destination coordinates |
| **Foursquare** | Local venue intelligence | Destination coordinates and category queries |

| **RevenueCat** | Subscription management and payment processing | Purchase tokens, subscription status |
| **PostHog** | Product analytics (anonymized) | Anonymized event data, no PII |

We do not share your personal information with any third party for advertising purposes.

---

## 4. Data We Do Not Collect or Sell

- We do not sell your personal data to anyone, ever.
- We do not share your data with advertisers or ad networks.
- We do not build behavioral profiles for targeted advertising.
- We do not track you across other apps or websites.
- We do not collect your contacts, photos, or calendar without your explicit permission for a specific feature.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your account and trip data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law (for example, payment records for tax purposes, which are retained for 7 years as required).

Anonymized, aggregated analytics data may be retained indefinitely as it cannot be linked to you.

---

## 6. Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data:

- All data in transit is encrypted via TLS.
- Passwords are never stored — authentication is handled via Supabase's secure auth system using email magic links or OAuth.
- API keys and secrets are stored server-side only and never exposed to clients.
- Row-level security policies ensure that your trip data can only be accessed by your account.

No system is perfectly secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at privacy@roamapp.app.

---

## 7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Access:** You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
- **Correction:** You can update your account information at any time within the app.
- **Deletion:** You can delete your account from the Profile screen. This removes your account, trip data, and all associated personal information from our systems within 30 days.
- **Data portability:** You can request an export of your trip data in JSON format by contacting privacy@roamapp.app.
- **Opt out of analytics:** You can opt out of anonymized PostHog analytics at any time in Settings > Privacy.
- **Withdraw consent:** Where we process data based on your consent, you can withdraw that consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@roamapp.app. We will respond within 30 days.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

ROAM is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at privacy@roamapp.app.

If you are accessing ROAM from outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country. By using ROAM, you consent to this transfer. We ensure that any such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws through appropriate safeguards.
