# ROAM App Store Optimization

## App Name (30 chars)
**ROAM: AI Trip Planner** (22 chars)

Rationale: "Trip planner" outranks "travel planner" in search volume. Colon separator is cleaner than em dash. "AI" front-loads the differentiator. Brand name first for recognition.

---

## Subtitle (30 chars)
**Plan any trip in 30 seconds** (29 chars)

Rationale: "30 seconds" is more specific and compelling than "60 seconds." Captures "plan," "trip" as secondary index terms. The claim is testable and creates a hook — users want to verify it. Avoids generic words like "smart" or "ultimate."

---

## Keywords (100 chars max, comma-separated, no spaces)
```
itinerary,vacation,backpacking,budget,solo,destination,explore,road trip,hidden gems,local,guide,Gen Z,spontaneous,vibe
```
(99 chars)

Rules applied:
- No repeats from name/subtitle (Apple indexes name + subtitle automatically)
- No spaces after commas (reclaims ~14 chars)
- No brand name (already indexed from title)
- No articles/fillers
- Mixed volume: "itinerary" (high), "backpacking" (medium), "hidden gems" (niche + trending)
- "Gen Z" targets a demographic search vertical
- "vibe" and "spontaneous" capture ROAM's brand-differentiating language
