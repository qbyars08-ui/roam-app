## Key Findings

- **Duolingo**: Deferred signup = 20% increase in DAUs. Users complete a real lesson BEFORE account creation
- **Notion**: Learn-by-doing inside the actual product. Templates reduce blank-canvas anxiety

- **BeReal**: Notification IS the activation. First product action happens during onboarding
- **Headspace**: 3 questions → immediate mini-meditation. Quick win in under 60 seconds
- **TikTok**: Content in 1 click vs Instagram's 8 minimum. Algorithm transparency gives agency
