## Why Micro-Influencers for ROAM?

Micro-influencers (5K-500K followers) deliver 3x higher engagement rates than macro creators, with typical engagement of 5-20% versus 1-3% for million-follower accounts. For a premium AI travel planner like ROAM, this means:

- **Higher trust**: Their audiences treat recommendations like advice from a friend
- **Better ROI**: Mid-tier TikTok creators (50K-500K) typically charge $500-$5,000 per post
- **Natural fit**: These creators actively plan trips and share itineraries -- exactly what ROAM automates
- **Authentic integration**: Smaller creators can weave ROAM into their real planning process, not just do a scripted ad read

## Partnership Strategy Summary

### Recommended Budget Allocation

| Tier | Creators | Est. Cost Per Post | Total Budget Range |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Tier 1** (100K-500K) | #1-6 | $1,000-$5,000 | $6,000-$30,000 |
| **Tier 2** (50K-150K) | #7-12 | $300-$1,500 | $1,800-$9,000 |
| **Tier 3** (Under 50K) | #13-20 | Product trade + $100-$500 | $800-$4,000 |
| **Total** | 20 creators | -- | **$8,600-$43,000** |

### Recommended Campaign Structure

1. **Phase 1 -- Seed (Weeks 1-2)**: Onboard Tier 3 creators with free ROAM Pro access. No payment, just product. Collect authentic feedback and early content.
2. **Phase 2 -- Amplify (Weeks 3-4)**: Launch paid partnerships with Tier 2 creators using "plan with me" format. Include unique discount codes for tracking.
3. **Phase 3 -- Scale (Weeks 5-8)**: Activate Tier 1 creators with polished campaign briefs informed by what worked in Phases 1-2. Use top-performing Tier 3 content as social proof in outreach.

### Content Formats That Work

- **"Plan with me" videos**: Creator uses ROAM live to plan a real upcoming trip
- **Before/after comparisons**: Hours spent planning manually vs. minutes with ROAM
- **AI vs. creator challenges**: Can ROAM's AI match a creator's expert itinerary?
- **"I let an AI plan my trip" series**: Multi-part content following the full trip lifecycle
- **Comment-driven trips**: Followers suggest destinations, creator plans with ROAM in real time

### Key Metrics to Track

- **Discount code redemptions** per creator (direct attribution)
- **App installs** from TikTok during campaign windows
- **Cost per install** by tier
- **Free-to-Pro conversion rate** from influencer-driven installs
- **Content engagement rate** vs. creator's average (did ROAM content over- or under-perform?)
