# ROAM — 30-Day Content Calendar

## Strategy

**Goal:** Build pre-launch buzz, grow the waitlist, and establish ROAM as the travel app for people who actually travel.

**Platforms:** TikTok (primary), Twitter/X (secondary)
**Posting cadence:** 1 TikTok/day, 1-2 tweets/day
**Best posting times:** TikTok 11am, 3pm, 7pm EST | Twitter 8am, 12pm, 5pm EST

**Content Pillars:**
1. **Product demos** (30%) — Screen recordings, feature reveals
2. **Travel tips** (25%) — Opinionated destination advice
3. **Relatable hooks** (25%) — Travel planning pain points, memes
4. **Build in public** (20%) — Founder journey, behind the scenes
