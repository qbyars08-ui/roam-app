# ROAM — Press Kit

## The One-Liner

ROAM is an AI travel planner that builds full, opinionated itineraries in under 60 seconds — the kind your most well-traveled friend would text you at 2am.

---

## What Is ROAM?

ROAM kills the 11-tab trip planning spiral. Tell it where you're going, your budget, and how you travel — and it hands you a complete day-by-day itinerary with named restaurants (and the exact dish to order), specific hotels, real costs, and insider tips that sound like they came from someone who actually lives there.

No "consider exploring the local cuisine." No "top 10 things to do in Paris." Just: "Get the khao soi at Khao Soi Khun Yai before 11am — after that, the line wraps around the block."

Users pick a destination, set a budget tier, choose their travel vibes (hidden gems, nightlife, local eats — 16 options), and ROAM generates a full trip plan they can save offline, share with friends, or export to their calendar.

## The Problem

Trip planning is still broken in 2026. People spend 8+ hours bouncing between travel blogs, Reddit rabbit holes, Google Maps, and booking sites — and still end up at the tourist trap restaurant with the laminated menu.

AI travel tools exist, but they all sound like they were written by a marketing intern who's never left the office. "Explore the vibrant culture" tells you nothing. "Order the #3 at the counter — skip the appetizers, they're frozen" tells you everything.

ROAM is the first travel app that's actually opinionated.

## Key Features

### Core
- **60-Second AI Itineraries** — Day-by-day plans with 3 time slots (morning, afternoon, evening), real venues, real prices, insider tips
- **AI Travel Chat** — Ask anything. Get answers that sound like someone who's been there, not a search engine
- **Spin the Globe** — Can't decide? Let fate pick your next trip
- **Offline PREP Mode** — Language survival kits, emergency data, cultural guides, packing lists — all available without WiFi

### Personalization
- **Travel Profile** — 8-dimension personality system (pace, budget style, crowd tolerance, food adventurousness, transport, accommodation, trip purpose)
- **AI Travel Persona** — 12 archetypes from "Midnight Explorer" to "Budget Ninja" — generated from your trip history, shareable as PNG cards
- **Made For You** — Destination recommendations based on your travel DNA
- **Trip Chemistry** — Group travel compatibility matching (bring the right friends)

### Gamification
- **Travel Passport** — Stamp collection and badge system for every destination
- **Year in Review** — shareable travel stats and your personal highlights
- **Visited Map** — Watch your world map fill in as you travel

### Safety & Practical
- **Emergency SOS** — Long-press to send GPS location to emergency contacts via SMS
- **Weather Intelligence** — 7-day forecasts with packing advice and best outdoor days
- **Budget Guardian** — Real-time spending tracker against your trip budget
- **Packing AI** — Personalized packing lists based on destination, weather, and activities

### Social
- **Shareable Viral Cards** — One-tap PNG export of any itinerary or persona card
- **Honest Reviews** — Community trip reviews from real travelers
- **Local Lens** — Neighborhood-level guides beyond the tourist zones

Quinn Byars built ROAM in two weeks, solo, as a high schooler. The app generates opinionated, specific travel itineraries using AI — and it's already more useful than apps backed by billion-dollar companies. The Gen Z founder story writes itself.

### 2. "The End of 11-Tab Trip Planning: How AI Is Finally Making Travel Planning Not Suck"
ROAM represents a new category: AI that's actually opinionated.
