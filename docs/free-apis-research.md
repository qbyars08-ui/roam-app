## Tier 1: Essential (High Impact, Strong Free Tiers)

1. **Amadeus** — Flights, hotels, POI, safety. 2K-3K req/month free. Industry standard
2. **OpenWeatherMap** — 1,000 calls/day. Already integrated in ROAM
3. **Google Places (New)** — 10K free calls/SKU/month. Gold standard for POI
4. **Mapbox** — 100K geocoding, 50K map loads/month free. Cheaper than Google at scale
5. **ExchangeRate-API** — 1,500 req/month. Simple REST, no SDK needed
6. **Pexels** — Completely free, 20K/month. Requires attribution
7. **Unsplash** — 50/hr demo, 5K/hr production. Best photo quality

## Tier 2: Very Useful

8. **Geoapify** — 3,000 req/day. OSM-based alternative to Google Places
9. **Google Translate** — 500K chars/month free forever
10. **DeepL** — 500K chars/month. Best quality for European languages
11. **Visual Crossing** — Historical weather back to 1970s. 1,000 records/day
12. **Ticketmaster** — 5,000 req/day. Concerts, sports, theater
13. **HERE Maps** — 250K transactions/month. Strong transit data
14. **Kiwi Tequila** — Free, earn commission. Best budget airline coverage
15. **US State Dept API** — Free, no limit. Travel advisory levels 1-4
16. **Tomorrow.io** — 500 calls/day. Hyper-local weather + air quality

## Tier 3: Useful Additions

17. **OpenTripMap** — 10M+ POI from OSM/Wikidata. Free for non-commercial
18. **Foursquare** — 10K/month free. Strong restaurant/nightlife data
19. **Nominatim/OSM** — Free geocoding. Self-hostable for unlimited
20. **Wikimedia Commons** — 100M+ free photos. Quality varies
21. **Transitland** — 2,500+ transit operators. Free GTFS data
22. **Rome2Rio** — Multi-modal transport. 100K searches/month free
23. **Passport Index Dataset** — MIT license. Visa requirements for 199 countries
24. **Eventbrite** — 500 req/day. Local/community events
