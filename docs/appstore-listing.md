## Description

ROAM is the AI travel planner that sounds like your most well-traveled friend, not a corporate chatbot.

Tell us where you're going, your budget, and your vibes. In under a minute, ROAM builds a complete day-by-day itinerary with real restaurants, actual activities, and insider tips you won't find on TripAdvisor.

**WHAT MAKES ROAM DIFFERENT**

- Real places, real prices. Every recommendation is a specific restaurant, a named hotel, a real street corner. No "explore the local area."
- Tips your friend would text you. "Order the #3, skip the appetizers" — not "consider visiting during off-peak hours."
- Built for how you actually travel. Backpacker hostels or boutique hotels. Solo adventures or date nights. We plan for YOUR style.

**KEY FEATURES**

- AI Itineraries — Full day-by-day plans with morning, afternoon, and evening activities, real costs, and accommodation picks
- Spin the Globe — Can't decide? Let fate pick your next destination with one tap
- Trip Dupe — Dream of Maldives on a Bangkok budget? We find the affordable alternative with the same soul
- Travel Passport — Collect stamps and badges for every destination you visit
- Travel Alter-Ego — Take a 5-question quiz to discover your travel personality type
- Calendar Export — One tap to add your entire trip to Apple Calendar
- Share Trips — Send your itinerary to friends with a single link
- AI Chat — Ask any travel question and get honest, specific answers
- Emergency SOS — Long-press for instant emergency contacts with your GPS location
- Offline Access — Your itineraries are saved for offline viewing

**PRICING**

- Free: 1 AI-generated trip per month
- Pro: Unlimited trips, priority generation

Download ROAM and plan your next obsession.
