# ROAM — Investor One-Pager

## The AI Travel Planner That Sounds Like Your Most Well-Traveled Friend

---

## Problem

Trip planning is a 3-hour chore that ends at a tourist trap. Travelers bounce between blogs, Reddit, Google Maps, and booking sites — then still don't trust their plan. Existing AI travel tools generate generic, brochure-quality output that nobody follows.

**The gap:** There is no AI travel product that is opinionated, specific, and actually useful. "Consider exploring local dining" is not a recommendation. "Order the pad see ew at Jay Fai, get there by 3pm before the line" is.

## Solution

ROAM generates complete, day-by-day itineraries in under 60 seconds. Users pick a destination, budget, and travel vibes — and get a plan with named restaurants, specific dishes to order, real hotel recommendations, exact costs, and insider tips.

The voice is the product. ROAM sounds like a friend who's been there, not a chatbot reading a Lonely Planet.

## Key Features
- AI-generated itineraries with real places and costs
- Spin the Globe (random destination picker — viral mechanic)
- Trip Dupe (budget alternatives to dream destinations)
- Travel Passport (gamified stamp collection)
- Calendar export, trip sharing, offline access
- AI travel chat for any question
