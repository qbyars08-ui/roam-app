# ROAM — Email Sequences

## Overview

Three email sequences covering the full user lifecycle:
1. **Waitlist Welcome** (5 emails) — Pre-launch nurture
2. **New User Onboarding** (3 emails) — First week after signup
3. **Win-Back** (3 emails) — Re-engage churned users

All emails follow ROAM's brand voice: specific, opinionated, warm, never corporate.

## Technical Notes

### Email Service Provider
Recommended: **Loops** (built for product emails) or **Resend** (developer-friendly)

### Trigger Events

| Sequence | Trigger | Delay |
|----------|---------|-------|
| Waitlist #1 | Waitlist form submission | Instant |
| Waitlist #2 | After Email #1 | 3 days |
| Waitlist #3 | After Email #2 | 4 days |
| Waitlist #4 | After Email #3 | 7 days |
| Waitlist #5 | Manual (pre-launch) | — |
| Onboarding #1 | App signup (auth.users insert) | Instant |
| Onboarding #2 | After Email #1 | 3 days |
| Onboarding #3 | After Email #2 | 4 days |
| Win-Back #1 | 30 days since last app open | Instant |
| Win-Back #2 | 15 days after Win-Back #1 (if still inactive) | 15 days |
| Win-Back #3 | 15 days after Win-Back #2 (if still inactive) | 15 days |

### Unsubscribe
All emails must include one-click unsubscribe. Win-back sequence stops permanently if user unsubscribes or opens the app.

### Merge Tags
- `{{first_name}}` — User's first name (from Apple Sign-In or manual entry)
- `{{email}}` — User's email
- `{{destination}}` — Last searched or planned destination
- `{{trips_count}}` — Number of trips generated
- `{{days_inactive}}` — Days since last app open
