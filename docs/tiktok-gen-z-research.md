# Gen Z TikTok Travel Trends — ROAM Feature Recommendations

*Generated March 2026*

## Viral Content Formats (1M+ views)

1. **Day-by-day itinerary carousels** — Most saveable format. Slide-through plans (Day 1/2/3) function as free travel guides
2. **Budget breakdown reveals** — "How much my trip actually cost" combines entertainment + utility
3. **Reality-show vacation edits** — Dramatic cuts, confessionals, serialized parts
4. **Destination dupe comparisons** — Side-by-side price/crowd reveals. 400K+ posts, Albania flights up 284%
5. **Faceless POV content** — 0.5x wide-angle, no face, viewer IS the traveler
6. **Plot-twist trip videos** — Accidents, wrong flights, chaos. One Tunisia mix-up hit 28M views

## Scroll-Stopping Moments

- **Price reveal** — "$12/night" over a stunning villa shot
- **Hidden gem reveal** — Slow pan to empty paradise beach + "No one knows about this place"
- **Before/after contrast** — Grimy alley opens into jaw-dropping rooftop bar
- **Dupe callout** — "This is NOT Santorini" over identical-looking water
- **Chaos moment** — Something genuinely, hilariously wrong
- **Cost comparison shock** — "$3,000 in Paris vs $300 in Albania"

## Gen Z Travel Trends

- **Slow Travel** — #slowtravel grew 330% in 2025. Depth over breadth, emotional resets
- **Destination Dupes** — 38% of travelers motivated by savings. Albania, Paros, Philippines
- **Main Character Energy** — Intentional dressing, custom playlists per city, protagonist narrative
- **Spontaneous Trips** — 1 in 8 Gen Z books last-minute. 74% inspired by TikTok
- **Experience Over Logistics** — Save on flights, splurge on food/activities
- **Hidden Gems Obsession** — 69% prefer places not on their feeds
- **Documentation as Experience** — 39% feel obligated to post, 50%+ Gen Z say sharing IS part of the trip

## 5 Features to Build

### 1. "The Receipt" — Shareable Trip Cost Breakdown Card
Auto-generated receipt-style cost breakdown after itinerary generation. Toggle Budget/Mid/Splurge tiers. ROAM-branded, screenshot-ready.

### 2. "Dupe Finder" — AI Destination Dupe Engine
Enter dream destination + budget → get 1-3 cheaper alternatives with side-by-side comparison cards (cost, crowd level, vibe match %).

### 3. "Main Character Itinerary" — Cinematic Moment Markers
AI flags 3-5 "main character moments" per itinerary: optimal time, shooting angle, what makes it cinematic, suggested caption. Exportable "Shot List" card.

### 4. "Trip Wrapped" — Post-Trip Shareable Recap Cards
Spotify Wrapped for travel. 5-card stack: overview → budget → personality label → percentile ranking → next trip suggestion. Swipeable, shareable.

### 5. "Chaos Mode" — Spontaneous Surprise Trip Generator
One tap → surprise weekend trip. Budget + departure city only. Destination hidden behind scratch-off/flip reveal. "Send to the Group Chat" with dare framing.
