### Support Page Content

**Page Title:** ROAM Support

**Hero Section:**
Need help with ROAM? We're here for you.

**FAQ Section:**

**How does ROAM generate itineraries?**
ROAM uses advanced AI to build personalized day-by-day travel plans based on your destination, budget, travel dates, and style preferences. Every recommendation references real places with real pricing.

**Is my first trip really free?**
Yes. Every ROAM account gets one free AI-generated trip per month. No credit card required. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited trips.

**How do I upgrade to Pro?**
Open ROAM, go to your Profile tab, and tap "Upgrade to Pro." Your subscription is managed through your Apple ID and can be canceled anytime in your device Settings under Subscriptions.

**Can I use ROAM offline?**
Yes. All saved itineraries are available offline. You need an internet connection to generate new trips or use AI chat.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Open your device Settings > tap your Apple ID > Subscriptions > ROAM. Tap Cancel Subscription. Your Pro access continues until the end of your current billing period.

**How do I delete my account?**
Open ROAM > Profile > Settings > Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.

**How does Emergency SOS work?**
Long-press the SOS button to instantly access local emergency numbers for your destination. ROAM can also share your current GPS coordinates via text message to your emergency contact.

## 8. Privacy Policy

The following should be hosted at your privacy policy URL (e.g. `https://roamtravel.app/privacy`).

---

**ROAM Privacy Policy**

**Effective Date:** March 11, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 11, 2026

ROAM Travel Inc. ("ROAM," "we," "us," or "our") operates the ROAM mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using ROAM, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

**1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT**

**1.1 Information You Provide Directly**
- **Account Information:** Email address, display name, and profile photo when you create an account.
- **Trip Preferences:** Destinations, budget ranges, travel dates, travel style preferences (vibes), and other inputs you provide when generating itineraries.
- **Chat Messages:** Text you send through the AI chat feature for the purpose of generating travel recommendations.
- **Emergency Contact Information:** Name and phone number of your designated emergency contact, stored locally on your device.

**1.2 Information Collected Automatically**
- **Usage Data:** App feature usage, session duration, screens visited, and interaction patterns. This data is collected in aggregate and used to improve the App.
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and app version.
- **Location Data:** Approximate location (city-level) for destination recommendations and weather information. Precise GPS location is only accessed when you explicitly use the Emergency SOS feature. Location access is never passive or continuous.

**1.3 Information from Third-Party Services**
- **Authentication Data:** If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address as authorized by you during sign-in.
- **Subscription Data:** Apple manages all subscription transactions. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but never see your payment details.

**2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION**

We use collected information to:
- Generate personalized travel itineraries and recommendations.
- Process your AI chat queries and return relevant travel information.
- Manage your account and subscription status.
- Track your travel passport stamps and badges.
- Provide weather, safety, and flight information for your destinations.
- Improve the App's features, performance, and user experience.
- Communicate important service updates or respond to support requests.
- Enforce our Terms of Service and protect against misuse.

We do NOT use your information to:
- Sell your personal data to third parties.
- Build advertising profiles.
- Make automated decisions that have legal or similarly significant effects on you.

**3. AI AND DATA PROCESSING**

ROAM uses artificial intelligence services to generate travel recommendations. Here is how your data interacts with our AI systems:

- **Anthropic (Claude):** Your trip preferences and chat messages are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate itineraries and chat responses. Anthropic processes this data under their data processing terms and does not use your inputs to train their models when accessed via their API. Data is transmitted encrypted and is not retained by Anthropic beyond the duration of the API request.
- **Data Minimization:** We send only the information necessary to generate your itinerary (destination, dates, budget, vibes, and chat context). We do not send your email address, name, or account information to AI providers.

**4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES**

We use the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Supabase** | Authentication, database, cloud functions | Account data, trip data, usage data |
| **Anthropic (Claude)** | AI itinerary generation and chat | Trip preferences, chat messages |
| **Aviationstack** | Flight tracking and status | Flight numbers (no personal data) |
| **OpenWeatherMap** | Weather forecasts | Destination coordinates (no personal data) |
| **RevenueCat** | Subscription management | Anonymous user ID, subscription status |
| **Apple (StoreKit)** | Payment processing | Managed entirely by Apple |

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies. We select services that maintain strong data protection standards and process data only as necessary to provide their service to us.

**5. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY**

- Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure hosted in the United States.
- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256).
- Database access is secured with Row Level Security (RLS), ensuring you can only access your own data.
- We implement authentication via secure JWT tokens with appropriate expiration.
- Saved itineraries are also cached locally on your device for offline access using encrypted local storage.
- We conduct regular security reviews of our infrastructure and access controls.

**6. DATA RETENTION**

- **Active Accounts:** We retain your data for as long as your account is active.
- **Deleted Accounts:** When you delete your account, we permanently remove all personal data within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated usage statistics may be retained.
- **Chat Messages:** AI chat messages are retained for 90 days to support conversation history, then automatically deleted.
- **Trip Data:** Saved itineraries are retained until you delete them or delete your account.

**7. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES**

**All Users:**
- **Access:** Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
- **Correction:** Update or correct inaccurate personal data through your Profile settings.
- **Deletion:** Delete your account and all associated data from Profile > Settings > Delete Account. You may also email us at privacy@roamtravel.app.
- **Data Portability:** Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
- **Opt-Out:** Disable optional data collection (such as usage analytics) in your app settings.

**California Residents (CCPA/CPRA):**
Under the California Consumer Privacy Act and California Privacy Rights Act, you have the right to:
- Know what personal information we collect and why.
- Request deletion of your personal information.
- Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. **ROAM does not sell or share your personal information.**
- Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.
- Correct inaccurate personal information.
- Limit the use and disclosure of sensitive personal information.

To exercise these rights, email privacy@roamtravel.app or use the in-app deletion feature. We will respond within 45 days.

**European Economic Area, UK, and Swiss Residents (GDPR/UK GDPR):**
Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to:
- Access, rectify, or erase your personal data.
- Restrict or object to processing of your personal data.
- Data portability.
- Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.
- Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Our legal bases for processing are: contract performance (providing the App's services), legitimate interest (improving the App), and consent (optional analytics).

To exercise these rights, email privacy@roamtravel.app. We will respond within 30 days.

**8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY**

ROAM is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@roamtravel.app.

**9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY**

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. For material changes, we will provide prominent notice within the App.

**10. CONTACT US**

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices:
