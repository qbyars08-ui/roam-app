# UGC Platform Research — DACH Creator Sourcing

Last updated: 2026-03-15

## Executive Summary

For ROAM's DACH launch, we need 10-15 German-speaking travel creators to produce TikTok/Reels content. Four major UGC platforms were evaluated. **Recommendation: Start with direct outreach (free) + Insense ($400/mo) once budget allows.**

---

## Platform Comparison

### 1. Billo
**Website:** billo.app

| Category | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Cost** | Per-video pricing: $99-$300/video depending on complexity |
| **Minimum Spend** | No minimum — pay per video |
| **Creator Pool** | 5,000+ creators globally |
| **Travel Niche** | Limited — mostly product/unboxing creators |
| **European Creators** | ~15% of pool, mostly UK. German creators very limited |
| **Turnaround** | 5-7 business days per video |
| **How It Works** | Submit brief → matched with creator → review draft → approve |
| **Quality** | Medium. Good for simple product demos, less so for travel storytelling |

**Verdict for ROAM:** NOT RECOMMENDED for DACH launch. Too few German-speaking travel creators. Better for physical product brands. Could work later for English-language global content.

---

### 2. Insense
**Website:** insense.pro

| Category | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Cost** | Platform fee: $400/mo (Growth plan). Creator fees separate ($100-$500/video) |
| **Minimum Spend** | $400/mo platform + creator costs |
| **Creator Pool** | 20,000+ creators, strong European presence |
| **Travel Niche** | Good — dedicated travel/lifestyle category with active creators |
| **European Creators** | ~30% of pool. German, French, Spanish, Italian well-represented |
| **Turnaround** | 7-10 days average, can negotiate rush delivery |
| **How It Works** | Post campaign brief → creators apply → select favorites → manage in-app |
| **Quality** | High. Marketplace model means creators compete for your brief |
| **Key Feature** | Direct Meta/TikTok ad integration — can whitelist creator handles for paid ads |
| **DACH Filter** | Can filter by language and country. German-speaking travel creators: ~200-400 |

**Verdict for ROAM:** BEST OPTION when budget allows. Strong European creator base, travel-specific creators exist, ad whitelisting is valuable for scaling. At $400/mo + ~$150/video avg, budget 5 videos/month = $1,150/mo total.

---

### 3. Trend.io
**Website:** trend.io

| Category | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Cost** | Package pricing: $500 for 10 videos (starter), $1,000 for 25 videos |
| **Minimum Spend** | $500 (10-video package) |
| **Creator Pool** | 3,500+ creators, primarily US-based |
| **Travel Niche** | Moderate — some lifestyle/travel creators, mostly product-focused |
| **European Creators** | Very limited (~5%). Almost no German-speaking creators |
| **Turnaround** | 10-14 days per batch |
| **How It Works** | Submit brief + product → Trend matches creators → bulk delivery |
| **Quality** | Medium-high for US product content, untested for European travel |

**Verdict for ROAM:** NOT RECOMMENDED for DACH. US-centric platform with negligible German creator presence. Good value for US market expansion later ($50/video at scale).

---

### 4. Minisocial
**Website:** minisocial.com

| Category | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Cost** | $1,700 for 25 videos (micro plan), $3,500 for 50 videos |
| **Minimum Spend** | $1,700 |
| **Creator Pool** | 10,000+ vetted micro-influencers |
| **Travel Niche** | Strong — lifestyle and travel are core verticals |
| **European Creators** | ~20%, growing. Some German creators but not the focus |
| **Turnaround** | 2-4 weeks for full campaign |
| **How It Works** | Fully managed campaigns — submit brief, they handle everything |
| **Quality** | High — fully managed means quality control is strong |
| **Key Feature** | Includes organic posting (creators post to their own feeds, 5K+ followers) |

**Verdict for ROAM:** GOOD BUT EXPENSIVE for current stage. The organic posting feature (creators post to their own audience) is extremely valuable. Best for when ROAM has $2K+/month UGC budget. The per-video cost ($68-70) is actually great at scale.

## Phased Strategy

### Phase 0: Now (Free — $0/mo)
**Direct Creator Outreach**
- Search TikTok/Instagram for German travel creators with 5K-50K followers
- DM 50 creators per week with collaboration pitch
- Offer: free Pro subscription + revenue share on referrals
- Target: 5 creators producing content for free/barter within 2 weeks
- Tools: TikTok Creator Marketplace (free), Instagram DMs, LinkedIn

### Phase 1: Month 1-2 ($400-800/mo)
**Insense Growth Plan**
- $400/mo platform fee
- Commission 3-5 videos/month from German travel creators
- Use TikTok Spark Ads to amplify best-performing organic content
- Budget: $400 platform + $400-600 creator fees = $800-1,000/mo

### Phase 2: Month 3-4 ($1,500-2,000/mo)
**Insense + Minisocial Hybrid**
- Continue Insense for performance UGC (ad-optimized)
- Add Minisocial campaign for organic reach (creators post to own audience)
- 10 videos/month total across both platforms
- Start A/B testing scripts, hooks, and CTAs

### Phase 3: Month 5+ (Scale based on CAC)
**Full UGC Engine**
- Top 3 creators on retainer ($300-500/mo each)
- Continuous Insense campaigns
- Quarterly Minisocial burst campaigns for organic reach
- User-generated content repurposing pipeline
