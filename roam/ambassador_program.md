# ROAM Ambassador Program

Last updated: 2026-03-15
Author: Agent 14 — UGC Engine

## Program Overview

ROAM's Ambassador Program converts active creators into long-term growth partners. Three tiers structured around video output and proven performance. Each tier unlocks higher revenue share, higher per-video rates, and deeper co-marketing access.

**Program philosophy:** We pay creators when they perform. No upfront fees for ROAM. No guesswork for creators. Revenue share is tracked in real time — creators see their own numbers in PostHog. Trust is built through transparency, not contracts alone.

## Revenue Share Mechanics

### How it works

Every ambassador gets a unique UTM link: `tryroam.netlify.app?ref=[creator_handle]`

When a user clicks that link, signs up, and upgrades to Pro:
- The subscription revenue is attributed to that creator in PostHog
- Revenue share is calculated on the **net subscription revenue** (App Store / Google Play fee deducted)
- Net revenue per Pro user: €9,99 × 0.70 (after store fee) = ~€7,00/month

### Revenue share calculation

| Tier | Net Revenue per User | Monthly Share per Active User |
|------|---------------------|-------------------------------|
| Seed | €7,00 | €0.70/user/month |
| Growth | €7,00 | €1.05/user/month |
| Partner | €7,00 | €1.40/user/month (lifetime, no expiry) |

**Example — Growth tier creator with 50 active referred subscribers:**
50 users × €1.05 = €52.50/month passive revenue share (in addition to per-video rate)

**Example — Partner tier creator with 200 active referred subscribers:**
200 users × €1.40 = €280/month passive revenue (on top of €200–500/video rate)

### Churn handling

If a referred user cancels their subscription, the revenue share stops for that user in the following month. No clawback on already-paid share.

### Annual subscriptions

If user purchases annual Pro (€79,99/year): revenue share calculated on full annual net revenue in month of purchase. Seed: ~€5,60 · Growth: ~€8,40 · Partner: ~€11,20

## Contract Outline

*Applies to Growth and Partner tiers. Seed tier operates on a simpler handshake agreement (no formal contract required for video 1–5).*

### ROAM Creator Agreement — Standard Terms

**Parties**
- ROAM (operator: [legal entity name, to be completed on execution])
- Creator: [Full legal name, address, tax ID]

**Effective Date:** Date of Tier 2 (Growth) activation

---

**Section 1 — Deliverables**

1.1 Creator agrees to produce a minimum of [X] videos per month as agreed at tier activation (minimum: 2/month for Growth, 4/month for Partner).

1.2 Each video must include:
- ROAM app demo footage (screen recording or over-the-shoulder)
- Reference to ROAM by name
- Creator's unique UTM referral link in bio or video caption for the duration the post remains live
- No competing AI travel app promotion in the same post

1.3 Videos must be published on TikTok and/or Instagram Reels within 7 days of receiving the content brief.

1.4 Creator will not delete published videos for a minimum of 90 days without written consent from ROAM.

---

**Section 2 — Compensation**

2.1 Per-video rate as specified at tier activation (see Tier Comparison above). Payment issued within 5 business days (Growth) or 3 business days (Partner) of video going live, conditional on video meeting brief requirements.

2.2 Revenue share percentage as per tier (Growth: 15%, Partner: 20%) applied to net subscription revenue from users attributed to Creator's referral link via PostHog tracking.

2.3 Revenue share paid on the last day of each calendar month covering the prior month's attributed subscriptions.

2.4 Payment method: PayPal, SEPA bank transfer, or Wise. Creator to provide banking details at onboarding. ROAM is not responsible for incorrect banking details provided by Creator.

2.5 ROAM reserves the right to withhold payment for videos that materially breach content guidelines (see Section 4). Creator will receive written notice within 48 hours of publishing if a video fails to meet guidelines.

---

**Section 3 — Content Rights & Usage**

3.1 Creator retains ownership of all produced content.

3.2 Creator grants ROAM a non-exclusive, royalty-free, worldwide license to:
- Repost the video on ROAM's owned TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube channels
- Use video in paid TikTok Spark Ads or Meta paid promotion (whitelisting)
- Feature video in ROAM's website, press materials, and pitch decks

3.3 ROAM must tag or credit Creator when reposting organic content. No credit required for paid ad whitelisting (industry standard).

3.4 Creator may not use ROAM's name, logo, or brand assets in commercial pitches to other brands or agencies without written consent.

---

**Section 4 — Content Guidelines**

4.1 Content must be authentic. No scripted, teleprompter-read, or obviously fabricated reactions.

4.2 Content must not be defamatory, misleading, or in violation of TikTok/Instagram community guidelines.

4.3 Content must comply with German and EU advertising disclosure requirements (Kennzeichnungspflicht): partnership content must be labeled as "Werbung" or "Anzeige" per applicable regulations.

4.4 Creator may not make claims about ROAM that are materially false (e.g., features the app does not have, false performance data).

4.5 Creator agrees not to promote direct ROAM competitors (AI travel planning apps) during the term of this agreement. Indirect competition (hotels.com, booking.com, Airbnb) is not restricted.

---

**Section 5 — Exclusivity (Partner Tier Only)**

5.1 Optional exclusivity rider available for Partner creators: ROAM pays a flat €200/month retainer in exchange for Creator not promoting any direct AI travel planning competitor.

5.2 Exclusivity is always opt-in. No exclusivity clause applies unless Creator explicitly agrees in writing.

5.3 Exclusivity period: minimum 3 months if activated. Either party may terminate exclusivity clause with 30 days written notice.

---

**Section 6 — Term & Termination**

6.1 Agreement is month-to-month. Either party may terminate with 14 days written notice.

6.2 Upon termination, all pending per-video payments owed at time of termination will be paid within 14 days.

6.3 Revenue share continues for 90 days post-termination for users already attributed to Creator's link at time of termination. After 90 days, revenue share ends regardless of whether referred users remain subscribed.

6.4 ROAM may terminate immediately (with no payment obligation for pending videos) if Creator materially breaches Section 4 guidelines.

---

**Section 7 — Confidentiality**

7.1 Creator agrees not to publicly disclose per-video rates, revenue share percentages, or specific performance data (signups, CTR) without written consent from ROAM.

7.2 ROAM will not publicly disclose Creator's personal financial information.

7.3 Both parties may reference the collaboration publicly (e.g., "I'm a ROAM Partner Creator" or "ROAM works with [Creator]") without prior approval.

---

**Section 8 — Governing Law**

8.1 This agreement is governed by the laws of [Austria / Germany — to be specified at execution] depending on the registered entity at time of contract.

8.2 Any disputes shall first be resolved via good-faith written negotiation. If unresolved within 30 days, disputes shall be submitted to arbitration in [Vienna / Berlin — to be specified at execution].

## Program Economics (Projected)

### Month 1 — 10 Seed creators
- Per-video cost: €0 (Seed tier = Pro account only)
- Pro account cost: 10 × €9,99/month = ~€100/month in foregone revenue
- Target signups: 10 creators × 5 videos × 50 avg views × 1% CTR = ~25 signups
- Target paid conversions at 5%: ~1–2 paid users
- Revenue share payout: negligible (<€2)
- Net cost: ~€100 in foregone Pro revenue

### Month 2 — 5 Growth + 10 Seed
- Per-video cost: 5 creators × 2 videos × €75 avg = €750
- Revenue share: 15 active creators × avg 10 referred paid users × €1.05 = €157.50
- Net cost: ~€750 − ~€157.50 = ~€592.50
- Expected signups: 500+
- Expected paid conversions: 25+
- Revenue from conversions: 25 × €7/month = €175/month recurring

### Month 3 — 2 Partner + 5 Growth + 10 Seed
- Per-video cost: 2 × €350 + 5 × €75 = €700 + €375 = €1,075
- Revenue share: growing pool of referred subscribers (est. 100 paid users total)
  - Partner 20%: 40 users × €1.40 = €56/month
  - Growth 15%: 40 users × €1.05 = €42/month
  - Seed 10%: 20 users × €0.70 = €14/month
  - Total revenue share: ~€112/month
- Net creator cost: ~€1,075 − €112 = ~€963
- Expected recurring MRR from creator-driven paid users: 100 × €7 = €700/month
- CAC via creator program: ~€963 / ~50 new paid conversions month 3 = ~€19/paid user (improving monthly as revenue share compounds)

## Program Rules — Non-Negotiables

1. **No payment before video is live.** Per-video rate is issued after video publishes, not before.
2. **Creators must use their UTM link.** No UTM = no revenue share tracking = no revenue share. Non-negotiable.
3. **Disclosure is mandatory.** German Werbung labeling is a legal requirement. ROAM will not work with creators who refuse to label sponsored content correctly.
4. **One active program per creator.** Creators cannot be in ROAM's program and a direct competitor's program simultaneously (applies to Growth and Partner tiers only).
5. **Transparency on both sides.** Creators see their real numbers. ROAM sees their real numbers. No hidden performance tiers or secret bonus structures.
