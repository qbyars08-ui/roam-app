## Description (4000 chars max)

Plan your next trip in 30 seconds. ROAM builds complete day-by-day itineraries using AI that thinks like a local, not a tourist.

Most travel apps give you a list of landmarks and leave you to figure out the rest. ROAM is different. Tell us where you're going, your budget, and the vibe you're after — and in 30 seconds you'll have a full itinerary with real restaurant names, real costs, exact transit directions, and a schedule that actually makes geographic sense. No more 47 open tabs. No more generic "top 10" lists. Just a trip that feels like it was planned by a friend who's lived there for years.

AI TRIP GENERATION
ROAM's AI builds complete multi-day itineraries in under 30 seconds. Every recommendation is specific: actual restaurant names with price ranges, real neighborhoods worth exploring, transit directions between stops, and time-of-day suggestions so you hit that ramen spot when the line is short. Watch your trip stream in real-time as the AI thinks through your perfect day.

LOCAL INTELLIGENCE
Forget surface-level city guides. ROAM operates at neighborhood-level specificity. Instead of "visit Shibuya," you'll get the exact backstreet izakaya three blocks from the station that locals go to after work. The AI understands the difference between tourist-friendly and tourist-trap, and it always picks the right side.

AUDIO GUIDE
Turn your itinerary into a narrated audio guide. Listen to your trip overview like a podcast while you pack, commute, or walk through a new city. Every stop comes with context — the history, the local significance, the reason this spot made the cut.

TRAVEL COMPANION FINDER
Traveling solo doesn't mean traveling alone. ROAM's Traveler Radar shows you other travelers headed to the same destination during your dates. Connect before you land, find travel partners for day trips, or just know there's a familiar community wherever you go.

OFFLINE EMERGENCY PREP
The Prep tab loads critical destination intelligence before you leave — and it all works without internet. Emergency numbers, embassy contacts, local currency info, safety advisories, and health briefings specific to where you're headed. Because the moment you actually need this information is usually the moment you don't have WiFi.

TRAVEL HEALTH INTEL
Get destination-aware health briefings before your trip. ROAM surfaces vaccination recommendations, water safety, altitude considerations, and region-specific health notes so you can prep properly and travel with confidence.

WHY TRAVELERS CHOOSE ROAM
- 30-second AI itineraries with real, specific recommendations
- Neighborhood-level local intelligence, not generic guides
- Audio narration for every trip
- Find other travelers headed to your destination
- Offline emergency info, currency, and safety data
- Built for the way Gen Z actually travels

ROAM is free to start — generate one full trip every month at no cost. Upgrade to ROAM Pro for unlimited trip generation and full access to every feature.

Join the early access community. The next generation of travel planning is here, and it fits in your pocket.
