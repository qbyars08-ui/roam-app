## App Features to Highlight — Updated Reference Sheet

What's live at tryroam.netlify.app as of 2026-03-15. Use this when briefing creators on what to show.

### Feature 01 — Trip Generation (core, always show)
**What it does:** Destination + budget + vibes → full day-by-day itinerary in ~30 seconds.
**How to film:** Screen record from app open → input → compass loader → itinerary reveal. Real-time, no editing tricks.
**What to zoom on:** Day-by-day plan, specific restaurant names, budget per day.
**Duration in video:** 20–30 seconds of demo is ideal.

### Feature 02 — Match Score Badge (NEW — show this)
**What it does:** Every TravelerCard on the People tab shows a "87%" or "94%" match score badge in sage green with a lightning bolt icon.
**How to film:** Open People tab → scroll to Matched Travelers section → zoom in on any card → match score badge is in top-right of the name row.
**Why it's content-worthy:** "94% match — how does it know?" is a genuine mystery hook. Viewers will comment asking how the algorithm works.
**Caption angle:** "Die App matcht dich mit 94% Genauigkeit auf andere Reisende. Ich weiß auch nicht wie."

### Feature 03 — Social Proof Counts (NEW — mention in voiceover)
**What it does:** Hero card on People tab shows: "2.4k Active travelers · 47 Destinations · 128 Groups forming."
**How to film:** Open People tab → hero card at top → stat row is visible immediately.
**Why it matters:** Real numbers make the app feel alive. "2.400 Reisende gerade aktiv" is a credible FOMO signal.
**Caption angle:** "2.400 Leute nutzen das gerade. Darunter bestimmt einer der zur gleichen Zeit an dein Ziel fährt."

### Feature 04 — Open Groups (show for People tab content)
**What it does:** Horizontal scroll of group trip cards — destination photo, "4 going", date range, vibe match label.
**How to film:** People tab → scroll down to "Open groups" section → swipe horizontally through Bali/Tokyo/Barcelona group cards.
**What to zoom on:** "4 going · May 1–10 · Adventure + Beach" — the specificity makes it feel real.
**Caption angle:** "Offene Gruppen die noch Plätze haben. Ich hab einfach reingeschrieben."

### Feature 05 — Pro Gate on Connect (show the paywall moment)
**What it does:** When a free user taps "Connect" on a TravelerCard, a Pro gate overlay appears with copy: "Direct messages are a Pro feature. Connect for real."
**How to film:** This is actually good content — the pro gate is a natural story beat. "Und dann hab ich getippt 'Connect' und... da war plötzlich dieser Screen."
**Why it's content-worthy:** The paywall moment is a genuine reveal. "Du kannst alle Profile sehen — aber schreiben kannst du erst als Pro-User" creates curiosity and FOMO.
**How to handle in brief:** Tell creators this is a feature they should mention honestly. "Pro kostet 9,99 €/Monat — aber ehrlich gesagt hab ich's gerne gezahlt."

### Feature 06 — Prep Tab (always show, especially for DACH)
**What it does:** Live data for any destination: weather forecast, air quality, emergency numbers, currency converter, local holidays.
**How to film:** Generate trip → tap Prep tab → scroll through data cards. Currency converter is especially visual.
**What to zoom on:** Emergency numbers for the destination country (shows the app actually prepared for real trips), currency rates (specific, changes daily = feels live).
**Caption angle:** "Alles was du wissen musst bevor du landest — in einer App."

## Performance Kill/Scale Rules

### Kill Rules (stop briefing creator for this script):
- 48h CTR < 0.3% on the UTM link
- 7-day views < 3,000 (indicates content didn't land, not just a slow start)
- 0 signups in first 7 days AND no engagement in comments about the app

### Scale Rules (order second video same day):
- 48h CTR ≥ 1.5%
- 7-day views ≥ 10,000
- 5+ signups attributed in first 7 days
- Comment section showing genuine questions about the app ("wie funktioniert das?", "was kostet das?")

### Creator Upgrade Rules (move from Seed to Growth tier):
- Creator completes 5 videos AND generates at least 1 paid referral → offer Growth tier (15% rev share + €50–100/video)
- Happens automatically — no negotiation needed. Just send the Growth tier offer with exact numbers.
