## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Stop googling. Start talking.

CRAFT mode is how ROAM plans your trip. Open the app, describe where you want to go, and just talk — tell ROAM what you like, what you hate, what your budget is, how long you have. ROAM's AI listens, asks follow-up questions, and builds your personalized travel itinerary in real time. No forms. No dropdowns. No generic templates. Just a conversation that ends with a complete trip plan.

CRAFT works because it doesn't hand you a list — it builds you an itinerary. Every restaurant has context: why it fits your style, what to order, when to go. Every neighborhood is explained before you land. Transit directions match where you're actually staying, not the city center. Timing accounts for what you want to do and how you like to travel. The result is a day-by-day plan that feels like it was built by someone who knows you and knows the city.

ROAM covers the full travel lifecycle: Dream, Plan, Go, and Remember. In Dream mode, you build a destination wish list and set savings goals for the trips you want to take next. In Plan mode, CRAFT builds your itinerary. In Go mode, you have everything you need on the ground. In Remember mode, your trips become stories worth keeping — not just photos.

When you're actually on the trip, ROAM works as your travel companion. I Am Here Now puts the full power of ROAM into a single tap — wherever you are, whatever time it is, ROAM gives you the right next thing to do, eat, or see. At 2AM in a foreign city, ROAM still knows what's open and what's worth it. Emergency contacts, local hospital info, and embassy numbers are always one tap away. Key parts of your itinerary are available offline, so a lost signal doesn't strand you.

After the trip, ROAM helps you remember it. Trip Wrapped turns your travels into a personal highlight reel — the places you went, the meals you loved, the moments worth saving. The Journal gives you a place to write about each day while the details are still fresh. Anniversary reminders bring back your best trips exactly one year later, so you never forget where you've been.

All of it is powered by real data. ROAM integrates with Google Places for ratings and photos, OpenWeather for conditions and forecasts, Foursquare for local venue intelligence, TripAdvisor for deeper review context, Perplexity for live AI-powered search, plus flight data, currency rates, and emergency services APIs. Your itinerary isn't a guess — it's built on what's actually there.

First trip is free, no credit card required. Pro unlocks unlimited trip generations for $7.99/month. Whether you're planning a solo trip across Southeast Asia or a weekend escape two hours from home, ROAM is the AI travel planner that actually plans the trip.
