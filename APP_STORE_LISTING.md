## Description (4000 chars max)

Most travel apps give you a list of the same 10 restaurants every other tourist already found. ROAM gives you the trip a local would actually plan for you.

ROAM uses AI to generate a complete, hyper-specific trip itinerary in under 30 seconds. Tell us where you're going, what you're working with, and what you're into. We handle the rest — restaurants, transit, neighborhoods, timing, day-by-day flow, everything.

This is not a trip planner with templates. This is not a chatbot that gives vague suggestions. ROAM generates a real itinerary with real specificity, then lets you watch it build in real time.

WHAT ROAM BUILDS FOR YOU

Every itinerary includes specific restaurant recommendations with context, not just names. Transit directions that actually match where you're staying. Neighborhood breakdowns so you understand the city before you land. Day-by-day plans with timing that makes sense. Local tips you would only know if you'd been there or talked to someone who had.

ROAM covers any destination worldwide. Weekend in Mexico City. Two weeks in Japan. A long weekend in a city two hours from home. It works regardless of trip length, budget, or travel style.

THE 5 TABS

Plan — where trips start. Enter your destination, budget range, and travel vibes. ROAM streams your full itinerary in real time, so you watch it build instead of waiting on a loading screen. Edit, regenerate, or ask follow-up questions to dial it in.

Pulse — local context for wherever you're going. What's happening, what's trending, what the vibe actually is right now. Not a Wikipedia summary. Real texture.

People — if you're traveling with others, coordinate here. See what everyone wants, surface conflicts, get to a plan everyone can live with.

Flights — search and track flights without leaving the app. No jumping between tabs in a browser. No losing your place.

Prep — everything you need before you leave and after you land. Packing lists tailored to your trip. Emergency info. Offline access to the things that matter when you don't have signal.

REAL-TIME STREAMING GENERATION

Watch your itinerary appear token by token as ROAM builds it. There's no spinner, no waiting room. You see the trip come together in real time, which means you can start reading and reacting while it's still generating.

SHARE CARDS

Generate shareable cards from any part of your itinerary. Post your day plan, your restaurant picks, or your full trip overview. Built for Instagram, TikTok, and anywhere else you're posting.

MULTI-LANGUAGE SUPPORT

ROAM works in English, Spanish, French, and Japanese. Generate itineraries in your language, share in your language, prep in your language.

FREE TIER AND PRO

The free tier gives you one full trip generation per month. That's enough to try everything and see exactly what ROAM builds. Pro unlocks unlimited trip generations, priority generation speed, and full access to every feature.

WHO ROAM IS FOR

Travelers who have wasted hours on trip planning and ended up with a mediocre itinerary anyway. People who want a starting point that's actually good, not generic. Anyone who travels internationally and needs local knowledge they don't have. Groups that can't agree and need something to break the deadlock. Solo travelers who want confidence in their plans without having to be an expert on every city.

ROAM is not another app in the travel category. It's the first one that actually plans the trip.
